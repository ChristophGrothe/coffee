enjoy coffee

The perfect place for everybody who wants to enjoy a coffee and / or wants to work on the laptop in a lovely atmosphere

We only offer highest quality coffee from our own roastery.

it's a circle

First, we are looking for coffee farmers in countries like ethiopia. It's important for us that they treat their workers with respect, pay them fairly and don't occupy children for work. In return, they can rely on us as a fairly paying and longterm customer.

Third, You can enjoy our coffee in form of a delicious Verlängerter, Cappuccino or Espresso. And Fourth, to close the circle, we use 5% of our total profit to pay our farmers, even if they had a bad season and could not supply us with coffee beans.

Second, the fresh beans are gently roasted in our bio-roastery in Graz. The result are 100% Arabica-beans.
